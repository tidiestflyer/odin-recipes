Hotpocket

By the end of this recipe, you'll have a molten hot Hotpocket!

Ingrediants

  • 1 hotpocket
  • Microwave
  • 3 Souls of the damned

Steps

  1. Let's start things off right by removing the skin from the hotpocket. We are of course referring to the plastic liner. You may save this for later.
  2. Place the hotpocket directly on the microwave plate without any form of protection or napkin. IMPORTANT: This ensures the next person who uses the microwave has a huge pile of hotpocket innerds to deal with.
  3. Set the timer to 33 minutes, and proceed to forget about the microwave for 2 days because you were drunk when you wanted this hotpocket.
  4. Enjoy!
