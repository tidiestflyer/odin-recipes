Tuna Fish Sandwhich

By the end of this recipe, you'll have a pixel perfect tuna sandwhich!

Ingrediants

  • Bread
  • Can of Tuna
  • Mayonaise
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Pickle Juice

Steps

  1. Start by draining the water out of a can of tuna and placing the left over contents in a bowl.
  2. Next, we'll grab our Mayo and for every can of tuna we do roughly 2 generious spoon fulls.
  3. For every can of tuna give yourself a couple shakes of salt and pepper. If you like more, you can add more to taste.
  4. Add some pickle juice for some tang. Don't go too crazy otherwise the tuna will get liquidy. Unless of course you like it this way.
  5. Mix the contents together with your spoon until fully incorporated.
  6. Place your bread of choice on a plate, and add your delicous Tuna to the bread.
  7. Add what ever else you may like. Some people like to add cheese, or pickles - it's entirely up to you.
  8. Enjoy!
