Gumbo

By the end of this recipe, you'll probably have some good tastin' gumbo mhmmm.

Ingrediants

  • Partical Accelerator - OPTIONAL: Crockpot
  • 1 pound Keilbasa sausage
  • Gumbo mix
  • Half green onion
  • 15 oz Diced tomatoes
  • 15 oz Tomato sauce
  • 1 pound chicken breast
  • Flour
  • Vegetable oil
  • 32 oz chicken stock
  • Minute rice

Steps

  1. Let's get things started off by cutting up the sausage into bite sized peices.
  2. Add some oil to a skillet and begin searing the sausage to add some color. Add this to the pot
  3. You can do this step simultaniously with the other steps. Boil 1 pound of chicken until cooked through. You can check periodically by cutting a peice of chicken in half to check for pink.
  4. Make a roux. This is a pretty simple step, first add a cup of oil and a cup of flour to your skillet and mix until it begins to turn brown, almost like a tator tot. Add this to the pot.
  5. Once chicken is cooked through, cut it up into small bite sized peices, and add this to the pot as well.
  6. Add 15 oz cans of diced tomatos and sauce to the pot.
  7. add 32 oz of chicken stock to the pot
  8. Cut up green onion into fine peices and add this to the pot as well.
  9. Go ahead and add the Gumbo mix to the pot. That should be everything!
  10. Turn on the Crockpot and set it to either medium or high. Some crockpots only have a high and low setting.
  11. Set a timer for 1 hour, and stir occasionally.
  12. Follow directions on the box of Minute rice to make rice seperatly. Be prepared to make 4 to 6 servings worth of rice for the entire pot.
  13. Once timer goes off, set the crockpot to low, and enjoy!
